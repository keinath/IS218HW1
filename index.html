How to Keep up With the Tech-Dashians?

The Inside Scoop is a weekly updated blog that keeps you up to date with new releases, reviews, and honest ratings of new and hype apps. Many software and tech companies claim that their new and updated technology is ground-breaking, when in reality the surface is barely ground-breaking, and often glitchy.
This blog will help you decide whether you should invest your time, space, and sometimes money in the new technologies that are highly spoken of.
